Consumer Price Index

Oh, you think you pay pretty well? You made it by just fine on $10/hour back in 1984? That's cute. Well here's the deal...back in 1984 this number was 100. That's the baseline. That means your $1 in 1984 costs 3$ now. That's how you can make sense of hte CPI metric. Think of it as measure of movement, with 100 as the baseline. That's how much the proverbial 'basket of goods' has increased in price since the baseline was set.
